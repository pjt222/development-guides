- Self-referential chain execution (alchemist analyzing the test framework that tests the alchemist) produces recursive insight — the transmute proposals for test-composition-integrity are directly relevant to this very test
- Alchemical vocabulary maintains coherence across a 3-stage chain without degrading — no vocabulary leakage or mixing of technical/alchemical language
- The Ore Test is the most portable extraction from this chain — "Could this concept exist in a completely different implementation?" works as a general abstraction quality check beyond alchemy
